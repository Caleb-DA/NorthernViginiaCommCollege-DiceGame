Game Rules

A pass line bet proceeds in the Game of Crap as follows: the first roll of the dice in a craps round is called the “come out roll.”

Round 0

A come out roll of 7 or 11 automatically wins, and a come out roll of 2, 3, or 12 automatically loses - End of Game

Round 1

If 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 is rolled on the come out roll, that number becomes the Point.

If the Point is rolled first, then the player wins the bet, or If a 7 is rolled first, then the player loses.

Repeat

The player keeps rolling the dice until either 7 to lose or the Point is rolled to win.
